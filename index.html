🏦 Request Bank Loan

Max $350, one standard loan per player. Repaid in 10 fixed installments (French amortization) at 1.2% per installment, starting the turn after you borrow. Each installment is mandatory before rolling. 💡 The loan brings cash in and creates a liability — record it yourself.

🌿 Request Green Loan

Max $350, one green loan per player. Subsidised 0.6% per installment (vs 1.2% standard), repaid in 10 fixed installments starting the turn after you borrow. It also UNLOCKS green retrofits (+30% rent). 💡 Cash in, a green liability up — record it yourself.

🌱 Green Retrofit

Invest in the sustainable upgrade of a property. The retrofit cost is 30% of the property price and is capitalised as a Green Improvement asset. Retrofitted properties earn +30% rent and are more resilient to climate events. 💡 You may retrofit any newly bought property, but only up to 30% of the properties you already owned before financing.

Select a property to see the retrofit cost.
